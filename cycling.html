Bad Motorists Tumblr

BMT is a tumblelog of a collection of GIFs and videos of bad motorist etiquette in Toronto.

I try to avoid bias and be as respectful as possible. As a result, I believe transparency is key in these logs and I will not downplay my faults nor exaggerate motorists’.

All footage shot with a helmet-mounted GoPro HERO2 camera at 30FPS, HD widescreen 1080p, and a 170° angle FoV.
