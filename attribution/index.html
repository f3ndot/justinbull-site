Attribution For my creative works licensed under Creative Commons

This page serves to explain my preferred format of attribution, since a vast majority of my written or creative works fall under one of the Creative Commons licenses.

Name

The attribution name should be Justin A. S. Bull, Justin Bull, or J. Bull (listed in order of most preferred).

URL / Link

The attribution should link or make reference to https://www.justinbull.ca or the URL of my original work. Both https:// and www. can be omitted if need be.

You can see how to properly attribute my work by reading Best practises for attribution
